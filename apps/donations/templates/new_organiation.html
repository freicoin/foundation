Non-profit registration form:

If you represent a non-profit organization fits the goals of the Freicoin Foundation you can submit a request to receive funds issued by the Freicoin Foundation according to the donations you receive through this web.

Your donors can use Freicoin or Bitcoin to make their donations, but the Freicoin Foundation will only dispense Freicoins. Thus you must provide a Freicoin address. If you know how to use an address in both networks you can leave the Bitcoin address field empty. Otherwise provide a separate address for each currency as nothing prevents your donors from giving you Bitcoin.
